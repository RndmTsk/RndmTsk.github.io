Collection and Use of Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify or contact a single person.

At no time does the application ask for personally identifyable information. All information added to the app is contained solely on the device.

What personal information we collect

When you create a nickname for a unit, the nickname, unit, and vehicle type are stored locally on your device and are not shrared outside of the application's secure sandbox. To remove the information, either delete the nickname or the entire app as necessary.

Protection of Personal Information

The security of your personal information is a very serious matter. To that end, the application has been structured to eliminate the sharing of personal information outside of the secure sandbox.
